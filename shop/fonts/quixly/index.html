About

Quixly is a distinctive typeface, made by using bold curves and varied thicknesses. Without serifs, it offers a clean and modern look, perfect for a small business or an interesting poster. As the first fully complete font by me, I'm really proud of it. I used my whole week of vacation + 2 more weeks working on this font, so any support is highly appreciated.
